Product Devices and Claims

Devices


The devices used for applying the anti-sneeze composition include:
  • Cotton swab or similar product that would allow delivery of the composition into the nose; packaged or sealed for distribution
  • Nose stick tube; composition compounded into solid material for spreading and is administered through tip of the tube
  • Different nose stick tube with liquid (water with minerals removed/ non-aqueous medium) in core, where composition is retained
  • Slightly different nose stick with capillaries for liquid flow
  • Nose stick with cap with absorbent material with composition; stick acquires composition from cap







Claims

    1. An anti-sneeze or anti-sneezing method for stopping, reducing, controlling, or managing sneezes consisting topically and manually applying a composition to the skin of nasal vestibules of a subject
    2. Nasal care method for personal or animal care, where composition is applied to vestibules
    3. A medicated device to transfer composition to vestibules
